School Rules & Regulations

Attendance Policy

Students must attend at least 90% of school days. Late arrivals beyond 15 minutes will be marked as tardy.

Dress Code

Students must wear the official school uniform at all times. No inappropriate attire is allowed.

Behavior and Conduct

Respect for teachers and peers is mandatory. Any form of bullying, violence, or misconduct will result in disciplinary action.

Electronic Devices

Mobile phones and electronic devices must be turned off during class. Unauthorized use will result in confiscation.

Homework and Assignments

All assignments must be submitted on time. Late submissions will receive a penalty unless justified.

Examination Policy

Cheating during exams is strictly prohibited and will lead to suspension or expulsion.

Library Rules

Students must maintain silence in the library. Books must be returned within the due date to avoid penalties.

Health & Safety

All students must adhere to safety procedures during emergencies. Participation in drills is mandatory.
